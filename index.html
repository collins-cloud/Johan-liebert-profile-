Who is Johan?

Johan Liebert is the central antagonist of Monster, a character defined by intelligence, charm, and absolute emotional void. Raised through trauma and psychological experimentation, Johan grows into a manipulative mastermind who destroys lives without lifting a finger. His character explores themes of identity, morality, and the frightening possibility that true evil can wear a kind face.
